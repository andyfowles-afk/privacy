Privacy Policy

We believe privacy should be simple to understand. This page explains exactly what information Spain Cost Light does—and does not—collect.

Effective date: July 2026

Application: Spain Cost Light

Developer: Ankaz Solutions

Introduction

Thank you for choosing Spain Cost Light.

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how the application uses, stores and protects information when you use Spain Cost Light.

Spain Cost Light has been designed to work with as little personal information as possible.

Information We Collect

Spain Cost Light does not require you to create an account, sign in or provide personal information in order to use the application.

The application does not collect your name, address, telephone number or payment information.

The application does not upload your personal settings to any external server.

Local Storage

Spain Cost Light stores certain preferences locally on your own device to improve your experience.

These preferences may include:

  • Language selection
  • Notification preferences
  • Display preferences

This information never leaves your device.

Notifications

If you choose to enable notifications, Spain Cost Light will use your chosen settings to generate tariff change reminders.

Notifications are generated from data available to the app and do not require personal information to operate.

Analytics

At the time of publication Spain Cost Light does not use third-party analytics services.

Advertising

Spain Cost Light does not display third-party advertising.

Third-Party Services

Spain Cost Light may retrieve publicly available electricity tariff information in order to display current and upcoming tariff periods.

No personally identifiable information is shared with these services.

Your Rights

Under applicable privacy legislation, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the right to know what personal information is collected about you.

As Spain Cost Light does not collect personal information for normal operation, there is generally no personal data held by Ankaz Solutions relating to your use of the application.

Changes to this Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect improvements to the application or changes in legal requirements.

Contact
