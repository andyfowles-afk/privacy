Privacy Policy – Spain Cost Light

Last updated: 14 July 2026

Introduction

Spain Cost Light is developed by Ankaz Solutions. The app helps users choose the best time to use electricity under Spain's PVPC tariff.

Information We Collect

Spain Cost Light does not collect, store, transmit or share any personal information. No account or login is required.

Local Device Storage

The app stores only your selected region, notification preferences, vibration preference and interface settings on your own device. This information never leaves your device.

Notifications

If enabled, local notifications are scheduled on your device. They are generated locally and are never sent to Ankaz Solutions.

Third-Party Services

The app does not use Google Analytics, Firebase, Crashlytics, AdMob or advertising/tracking SDKs.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes

This policy may be updated from time to time. The latest version will always be published here.
