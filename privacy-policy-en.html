Privacy Policy

Power Price

Effective Date: 1 January 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Power Price ("the App") collects, uses and protects your information. Power Price has been developed by Ankaz Solutions.

Information We Collect

Power Price is designed to respect your privacy. The App does not require you to create an account and does not collect personally identifiable information.

The App may use the following information locally on your device:

  • Your selected language preference.
  • Your selected notification preferences.
  • Your selected electricity region.
  • Your theme preference (Light, Dark or Auto).

These settings remain stored only on your own device and are not transmitted to Ankaz Solutions.

Electricity Price Data

Power Price retrieves publicly available electricity tariff information to display current and upcoming tariff periods. This information does not identify you personally.

Notifications

If you choose to enable notifications, Power Price schedules notification reminders locally on your device. Notification schedules are not uploaded or shared with Ankaz Solutions.

Location

Power Price does not access your GPS location. The selected electricity region is chosen manually by you.

Analytics

Power Price does not currently use analytics services to monitor user behaviour.

Advertising

Power Price does not display advertising.

Third-Party Services

The App may access publicly available electricity tariff data provided by external services. Those services operate under their own privacy policies.

Data Sharing

AF Solutions does not sell, rent or share your personal information with third parties.

Data Storage

Your preferences are stored locally on your device using standard Android storage mechanisms. No user account information is stored on external servers.

Children's Privacy

Power Price is not directed toward children under the age of 13. The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Rights

Depending on your country or region, you may have rights regarding your personal information under applicable privacy laws including the UK GDPR and EU GDPR. Because Power Price does not collect personal information, there is generally no personal data held by AF Solutions to access, modify or delete.

Security

Reasonable measures are taken to protect the information stored within the App. However, no method of electronic storage can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Changes to this Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The latest version will always be available from this page.
