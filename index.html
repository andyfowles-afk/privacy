Our First Application

Spain Cost Light

A simple visual guide to Spain's regulated electricity tariff, helping households understand the cheapest times to use electricity throughout the day.

Why we built it

Spain's electricity tariff is accurate but often difficult to understand. Spain Cost Light presents the official tariff periods using clear colours, straightforward layouts and optional reminders, making everyday decisions much easier.

  • ✓ Colour-coded tariff periods
  • ✓ Daily tariff schedule
  • ✓ Optional smart reminders
  • ✓ Designed specifically for Spain
